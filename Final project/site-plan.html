Purpose

My purpose is to allow people to look up common mobs in DND and to maybe have them make there own Monster, I will have things listed about the mobs and you will be able to filter it as well in the search bar.

Audience

my target would be young people and people who are into DND.

Dynamic elements

I would use the search bar and filter, Using event listeners to here for click to generate the mob, maybe allowing the user to save there mob in a PDF or a link file? and adding a libary to and maybe a random generator.
